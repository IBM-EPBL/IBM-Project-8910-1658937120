A nutrition specialist known as a nutrition assistant uses diagnostic techniques to spot nutritional deficiencies in patients.
To ensure that patients receive the right nutrition, they collaborate closely with dietitians and nutritionists.
After evaluating all risk factors, nutritionists must interview their patients to ascertain their needs before providing the best meal plans.
